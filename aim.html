Movement Tips

First, avoid running in straight lines and strafe left or right to make yourself harder to hit. Use cover wisely by peeking, shooting, and quickly returning to safety instead of staying exposed. When entering a fight, slide to catch enemies off guard and break their aim. In close-range battles, add jumps or drop-shots to throw off enemy tracking. Finally, always mix up your movement by combining strafing, sliding, and jumping so your playstyle stays unpredictable.
